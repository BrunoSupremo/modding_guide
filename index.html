Stonehearth's Modding Guide

Welcome to the Stonehearth modding guide!

This is still work in progress, so there are many missing pages and not all the links work yet.

The modding guide is a "how-to" approach to modding, step by step. We've divided it in several groups, from more basic to more advanced. We'll also include explanations about the different types of scripts that we can find in the game.

We'll be updating the modding guide in the future to cover more content and fixing styling / formatting issues. Right now, the essentials (make sure to read them before attempting advanced stuff!), the basics, and a small part of the art guide are finished.

Happy modding!
